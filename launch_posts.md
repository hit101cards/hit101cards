I just released a small browser card game called Hit101.
You take turns playing cards to grow a shared total — hit exactly 101 and you win, 102+ and you bust.
Special cards (8 = skip, 9 = reverse, 10 = ±10, Joker = 50 or 1) make for some dramatic comebacks.
